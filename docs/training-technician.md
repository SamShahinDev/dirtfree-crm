## Role Overview

As a **Technician**, you are the face of Dirt Free Carpet in the field. Your responsibilities include:

- **Service Delivery**: Providing high-quality carpet cleaning services
- **Customer Interaction**: Professional communication and service excellence
- **Job Documentation**: Updating job status and capturing service photos
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring work meets company standards
- **Equipment Management**: Maintaining and reporting on vehicle/equipment status
- **Communication**: Keeping dispatch and customers informed of progress

### Key Access & Permissions

✅ **Personal Schedule**: View your assigned jobs and calendar
✅ **Job Updates**: Update status, add notes, and complete jobs
✅ **Photo Uploads**: Document work with before/after photos
✅ **Vehicle Reports**: Report vehicle needs and issues
✅ **Customer Communication**: Professional interaction guidelines

❌ **Other Technician Jobs**: Cannot see jobs assigned to other technicians
❌ **Customer Management**: Cannot create or edit customer information
❌ **Job Assignment**: Cannot assign jobs to yourself or others
❌ **System Configuration**: Cannot modify system settings

## Job Status Management

### Status Workflow

**Job Status Progression:**
1. **Scheduled** → Job assigned to you
2. **On the Way** → You've started traveling to job
3. **Arrived** → You've arrived at customer location
4. **In Progress** → Work has begun
5. **Completed** → Service finished successfully

### Updating Job Status

**"On the Way" Process:**
1. Review job details and customer information
2. Load equipment and prepare vehicle
3. Tap **"On the Way"** button in app
4. System automatically sends notification to customer
5. Begin navigation to customer location

**"Arrived" Status:**
1. Arrive at customer location
2. Tap **"Arrived"** button
3. Review job requirements with customer
4. Set up equipment and begin work

**"In Progress" Updates:**
1. Start service work
2. Take "before" photos if required
3. Document any additional services needed
4. Communicate with customer throughout process

**"Completed" Process:**
1. Finish all service work
2. Take "after" photos
3. Review work with customer
4. Collect payment if required
5. Tap **"Complete Job"**
6. System triggers customer survey

### Adding Job Notes

**When to Add Notes:**
- Additional services performed
- Issues encountered during service
- Customer requests or feedback
- Equipment problems or needs
- Access or scheduling difficulties

**Note Best Practices:**
- Be specific and detailed
- Use professional language
- Include relevant times and measurements
- Document any customer communications
- Report safety or access issues

**Adding Notes in App:**
1. Open job details
2. Tap **"Add Note"**
3. Select note type (service, issue, customer, etc.)
4. Enter detailed information
5. Save note (syncs with dispatch immediately)

## On the Way Workflow

### Pre-Departure Checklist

**Before Leaving for Job:**
- [ ] Review job details and customer information
- [ ] Check equipment and supply requirements
- [ ] Verify vehicle fuel and condition
- [ ] Load necessary equipment and chemicals
- [ ] Check GPS directions and estimated travel time
- [ ] Update job status to "On the Way"

### Customer Notification

**Automatic Notifications:**
- Customer receives SMS when you mark "On the Way"
- Message includes your name and estimated arrival time
- Customer can reply with questions or concerns
- System tracks delivery and response

**Sample "On the Way" Message:**
```
Hi [Customer Name]! This is [Your Name] from Dirt Free Carpet.
I'm on my way to your location for your scheduled carpet cleaning.
ETA: [Time]. Contact us at (555) 123-4567 if needed.
```

### Arrival Procedures

**Upon Arrival:**
1. **Mark "Arrived"** in app
2. **Assess parking** and equipment access
3. **Knock/ring doorbell** and introduce yourself
4. **Review service scope** with customer
5. **Conduct walkthrough** of areas to be cleaned
6. **Discuss any concerns** or special requests
7. **Begin equipment setup**

**Customer Interaction:**
- Professional appearance and demeanor
- Clear communication about service process
- Respect for customer property and privacy
- Address questions and concerns promptly
- Maintain clean and organized work area

## Photo Documentation

### Photo Requirements

**Required Photos:**
- **Before Photos**: Document initial condition
- **During Photos**: Show work in progress (optional)
- **After Photos**: Document completed work
- **Issue Photos**: Document any problems or additional services needed

**Photo Standards:**
- High resolution and clear focus
- Good lighting (use flash if needed)
- Multiple angles of each area
- Include reference objects for scale
- Avoid personal items or sensitive information

### Taking Photos with App

**Photo Capture Process:**
1. Open current job in app
2. Tap **"Add Photos"**
3. Select photo type (Before/During/After)
4. Use app camera to take photos
5. Review and retake if needed
6. Add captions or descriptions
7. Upload immediately or when connected

**Photo Organization:**
- Photos automatically linked to current job
- Organized by type and timestamp
- Available to dispatch and customers
- Stored securely with EXIF data removed
- Accessible for future reference

### EXIF Data Protection

**Automatic Privacy Protection:**
- Location data automatically removed from photos
- Device information stripped from metadata
- Timestamp preserved for job documentation
- Customer privacy maintained
- Compliance with privacy regulations

**Best Practices:**
- Focus on work areas, not personal belongings
- Avoid capturing sensitive information
- Get customer permission for before photos
- Use photos for documentation and quality purposes
- Report any privacy concerns to management

## Customer Surveys

### Survey Process Overview

**Automatic Survey Trigger:**
- Survey link sent via SMS after job completion
- Customer receives link within 15 minutes
- Survey tracks job satisfaction and feedback
- Results used for quality improvement

**Survey Content:**
- Overall satisfaction rating (1-5 stars)
- Service quality assessment
- Technician performance feedback
- Likelihood to recommend
- Additional comments or suggestions

### Survey Link Management

**Survey Token Security:**
- Unique, secure token for each job
- Time-limited link (expires after 30 days)
- Cannot be used multiple times
- Protects customer privacy and data

**If Customer Reports Issues:**
- Invalid or expired survey links
- Difficulty accessing survey
- Technical problems with survey
- **Action**: Contact dispatch immediately for resolution

### Post-Survey Actions

**Positive Feedback (4-5 stars):**
- Customer receives review site links (Google, Yelp)
- Encourages public reviews for business growth
- Technician recognition for excellent service

**Negative Feedback (1-3 stars):**
- Management immediately notified
- Follow-up contact with customer
- Investigation and resolution process
- Opportunity for service recovery

**Using Feedback:**
- Review your performance regularly
- Learn from customer comments
- Implement improvement suggestions
- Celebrate positive feedback
- Address concerns proactively

## Troubleshooting

### App Issues

**App Won't Load or Crashes:**
1. Close and restart app
2. Check internet connection
3. Restart mobile device
4. Update app if new version available
5. Contact dispatch if problem persists

**Cannot Update Job Status:**
1. Check internet/cellular connection
2. Try again in a few minutes
3. Use WiFi if available
4. Call dispatch to report status by phone
5. Update app when connection restored

**Photos Won't Upload:**
1. Check storage space on device
2. Try connecting to WiFi
3. Reduce photo size if needed
4. Retry upload later
5. Save photos and upload at end of day

### Customer Issues

**Customer Not Home:**
1. Call customer phone number
2. Wait reasonable time (10-15 minutes)
3. Leave note or message
4. Contact dispatch for instructions
5. Document attempt in job notes

**Access Problems:**
1. Contact customer for access instructions
2. Check for alternative entry points
3. Contact property manager if applicable
4. Call dispatch for guidance
5. Reschedule if access cannot be gained

**Customer Complaint:**
1. Listen carefully and stay calm
2. Apologize for any inconvenience
3. Try to resolve issue if possible
4. Contact dispatch immediately
5. Document incident thoroughly

### Equipment Problems

**Equipment Malfunction:**
1. Stop using equipment immediately
2. Check for obvious issues (power, connections)
3. Try basic troubleshooting steps
4. Contact dispatch for guidance
5. Use backup equipment if available

**Vehicle Problems:**
1. Pull over safely if driving
2. Assess problem severity
3. Contact dispatch immediately
4. Follow emergency procedures
5. Document incident and location

**Chemical Issues:**
1. Stop work immediately
2. Ventilate area if needed
3. Check product labels and safety data
4. Contact dispatch for guidance
5. Seek medical attention if exposure occurs
