## Common Questions to Address

**Q: How often does data update?**
A: Most metrics update in real-time. Some complex calculations refresh every 15 minutes.

**Q: Can I export any data?**
A: Yes! Every view has an export button for CSV, Excel, or PDF.

**Q: How do I compare different time periods?**
A: Use the date range selector to choose current vs. previous period.

**Q: What's a good conversion rate?**
A: For opportunities, 20-30% is industry standard. For promotions, 8-15% is strong.
