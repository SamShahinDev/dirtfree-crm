# Video Script: Review Management

**Duration**: 5-7 minutes
**Target Audience**: All staff, especially customer service and managers
**Prerequisites**: Basic CRM navigation

---

2. Why Reviews Matter (0:30 - 1:00)

#### Responding to Positive Reviews (3:15 - 3:50)

**[CLICK: A 5-star review]**
**[SCREEN: Review detail modal]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Let's respond to this positive review first. Always acknowledge 5-star reviews - it shows you care and encourages more reviews."

**[CLICK: "Respond" button]**

**[SCREEN: Response text area appears]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Keep responses authentic and personal. Use the customer's name and mention specific details from their review."

**[TYPE:]**
```
Thank you so much, Jennifer! We're thrilled you're happy with your carpet cleaning. Our team takes pride in delivering exceptional results. We appreciate your business and look forward to serving you again soon!
```

**[CLICK: "Post Response" button]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"There - a professional, warm response that took 30 seconds. Do this for every positive review!"

#### Responding to Negative Reviews (3:50 - 4:45)

**[SCREEN: Reviews dashboard]**
**[CLICK: A 2-star review]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Now the tough one - a negative review. Don't panic! This is an opportunity to show other potential customers how you handle problems."

**[SHOW: Review text saying "Service was okay but price was higher than expected"]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"The golden rules for negative reviews: Stay calm. Don't get defensive. Acknowledge their concern. Apologize if appropriate. Offer to make it right. Take it offline."

**[CLICK: "Respond" button]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Here's how we respond professionally:"

**[TYPE:]**
```
Thank you for your feedback, Robert. We're sorry your experience didn't meet expectations. We strive for transparency in pricing and clearly want to address your concerns. Please contact our manager directly at 555-1234 so we can make this right. We value your business and want to ensure you're satisfied.
```

**[HIGHLIGHT: Key phrases]**
- "Thank you for your feedback" (shows maturity)
- "We're sorry" (empathy without admitting fault)
- "Contact our manager directly" (takes conversation offline)
- "Make this right" (shows commitment to resolution)

**[CLICK: "Post Response" button]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Perfect. This response shows potential customers that you take feedback seriously and are willing to fix problems. That builds trust."

#### Response Best Practices (4:45 - 5:00)

**[TEXT OVERLAY: "Response Best Practices"]**

**[TEXT CALLOUTS:]**
- ✓ Respond within 24 hours
- ✓ Use customer's name
- ✓ Be genuine and specific
- ✓ Thank all reviewers
- ✓ For negatives: empathize, take offline, offer resolution
- ✗ Never argue or get defensive
- ✗ Don't ask to remove or edit review

## Common Questions to Address

Include these in FAQ or extended version:
- "What if customer threatens negative review?" (Offer to resolve, document everything, don't offer incentives to remove)
- "Can we incentivize reviews?" (No - violates most platform policies)
- "How to handle fake reviews?" (Flag through platform, respond professionally, don't accuse publicly)
- "Should we ask unhappy customers for reviews?" (No - fix the issue first, then ask later)
