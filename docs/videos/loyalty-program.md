## Common Questions to Address

**Q: Can customers drop down tiers?**
A: No, tiers are lifetime achievements. Once you reach Gold, you stay Gold even if you don't earn more points.

**Q: Do points expire?**
A: No, points never expire in the Dirt Free program.

**Q: Can I customize tier benefits?**
A: Yes! Admins can adjust point thresholds, earning rates, and tier perks in settings.

**Q: What happens with refunds?**
A: Points earned from refunded transactions are automatically deducted to prevent abuse.
