# Video Script: Creating Promotional Campaigns

**Duration**: 6-8 minutes
**Target Audience**: Marketing staff, managers, office personnel
**Prerequisites**: Basic CRM navigation

---

## Video Outline

1. Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
2. Promotion Types & Strategy (0:30 - 1:30)
3. Creating a Promotion Campaign (1:30 - 4:30)
4. Tracking Performance (4:30 - 6:00)
5. Best Practices & Tips (6:00 - 7:00)
6. Conclusion (7:00 - 7:30)

---

## Full Script

### INTRO (0:00 - 0:30)

**[SCREEN: Dashboard]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Promotional campaigns are one of the fastest ways to drive repeat business and stay top-of-mind with your customers. In this video, you'll learn how to create targeted promotional campaigns, deliver them via SMS and email, and track their performance. Let's dive in!"

**[TEXT OVERLAY: "Creating Promotions - Drive Repeat Business Automatically"]**

---

### SECTION 1: PROMOTION TYPES & STRATEGY (0:30 - 1:30)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Promotions page]**
**[CLICK: Promotions in sidebar]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Dirt Free CRM supports several proven promotion types, each designed for different customer scenarios."

**[SCREEN: Show promotion type examples]**

**[TEXT CALLOUTS appear:]**
- **Win-Back**: Re-engage customers who haven't booked in 6+ months
- **Birthday**: Special birthday month offers - everyone loves birthday perks!
- **Seasonal**: Spring cleaning, holiday specials, summer prep campaigns
- **Anniversary**: Celebrate customer relationship milestones
- **Referral**: Encourage customers to refer their friends

**VOICEOVER:**
"The key to successful promotions is targeting the right customers with the right message at the right time. Let's create a win-back campaign together."

---

### SECTION 2: CREATING A PROMOTION CAMPAIGN (1:30 - 4:30)

**[SCREEN: Promotions dashboard]**
**[CLICK: "+ New Promotion" button]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Click 'New Promotion' to get started."

**[SCREEN: Promotion creation form]**

#### Step 1: Basic Details (1:30 - 2:00)

**VOICEOVER:**
"First, give your promotion an internal name. This is just for your team - customers won't see it."

**[TYPE: "Spring Win-Back 2024" in promotion name field]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Select the promotion type. This helps with analytics and tracking."

**[CLICK: Type dropdown]**
**[SELECT: "Win-Back"]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Now describe your offer clearly and compellingly."

**[TYPE: "Come back this spring! Get 25% off any carpet cleaning service when you book before April 30th."]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Set your discount amount and valid period."

**[TYPE: "25" in discount percentage]**
**[SELECT: Start date and end date]**

#### Step 2: Target Audience (2:00 - 3:00)

**[SCREEN: Scroll to audience section]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Here's where the magic happens - targeting. Instead of blasting everyone, we'll send to specific customer segments."

**[CLICK: "Segment" radio button instead of "All Customers"]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"For a win-back campaign, we want customers who haven't booked recently. Select 'Last service more than 6 months ago.'"

**[CLICK: Segment dropdown]**
**[SELECT: "Last service > 6 months"]**

**[SCREEN: Show estimated reach number update]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"See that? The system shows we'll reach 347 customers who match this criteria. Perfect targeting means better results and lower costs."

**[OPTIONAL: Show additional filters]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"You can also filter by location, service type, or customer value. For now, let's stick with our 6-month inactive filter."

#### Step 3: Message Content (3:00 - 4:00)

**[SCREEN: Message content section]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Now let's craft our messages. We'll create both SMS and email versions."

**[CLICK: SMS tab]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"For SMS, keep it short and actionable. Remember, 160 characters is one message."

**[TYPE in SMS field:]**
```
Hi [FirstName]! We miss you! Come back this spring & save 25% on carpet cleaning. Book by 4/30. Reply YES or call 555-1234. -Dirt Free
```

**[SHOW: Character counter showing "156 characters"]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Perfect! Under 160 characters, includes personalization, clear offer, expiration date, and easy response options."

**[CLICK: Email tab]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"For email, we have more room for creativity and detail."

**[TYPE in subject line:]**
```
[FirstName], We've Missed You! 25% Off Inside
```

**[SCREEN: Rich text editor for email body]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Use the rich text editor to format your email. Include an eye-catching header, clear offer details, and a prominent call-to-action button."

**[SHOW: Pre-formatted email template being selected]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Pro tip: Use one of our pre-built templates to save time and ensure mobile responsiveness."

**[HIGHLIGHT: Personalization tokens dropdown]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Notice the personalization tokens - these automatically insert customer-specific information like first name, last service date, or lifetime value."

#### Step 4: Delivery Settings (4:00 - 4:30)

**[SCREEN: Delivery settings section]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Choose your delivery method. SMS has higher open rates at 95%, but email is more cost-effective and allows richer content. For maximum impact, use both."

**[SELECT: "Both SMS & Email" checkbox]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Timing matters! For optimal engagement, send Tuesday through Thursday between 10am and 2pm local time."

**[SELECT: "Schedule for later"]**
**[SELECT: Next Tuesday, 10:00 AM]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Perfect! Our promotion is scheduled for Tuesday at 10am - prime time for customer engagement."

**[SCREEN: Show estimated cost]**
**[HIGHLIGHT: "$87 estimated delivery cost"]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"The system shows estimated delivery cost based on your recipient count and selected channels. Always good to know before hitting send!"

#### Step 5: Review & Send (4:30 - 4:45)

**[CLICK: "Preview" button]**
**[SCREEN: Preview modal showing both SMS and email versions]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Always preview before sending. Check that personalization tokens work correctly and formatting looks good."

**[CLOSE preview]**
**[CLICK: "Schedule Promotion" button]**

**[SCREEN: Success message with confirmation]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"And we're scheduled! The promotion will send Tuesday at 10am, reaching 347 customers who haven't used our services in over 6 months."

---

### SECTION 3: TRACKING PERFORMANCE (4:45 - 6:00)

**[SCREEN: Navigate back to Promotions dashboard]**

"Now let's look at how to track promotion performance. Click on any active or completed campaign."

**[CLICK: An example campaign that's already run]**

"38% open rate for email is excellent - well above industry average. And 22% of SMS recipients responded!"

**[HIGHLIGHT: Revenue metrics section]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"But what really matters is revenue. Did the promotion drive bookings?"

**[SHOW: Bookings: 28, Revenue: $6,720, ROI: 773%]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Amazing! 28 bookings generating $6,720 in revenue from an $87 investment. That's a 773% return on investment."

**[SCREEN: Scroll to customer responses section]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"You can even see individual customer responses. These SMS replies show real customer engagement."

**[SHOW: Customer responses like "YES PLEASE!", "What times available?", "Book me for Friday"]**

---

### SECTION 4: BEST PRACTICES & TIPS (6:00 - 7:00)

**[SCREEN: Generic promotions overview]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Let me share some proven best practices for promotional success."

**[TEXT OVERLAY: "Best Practices"]**

**[TEXT CALLOUTS appear one by one:]**

**1. Don't Over-Message**
**VOICEOVER:**
"Send no more than 1-2 promotions per month. Too many messages and customers tune out or opt out."

**2. Segment Your Audience**
**VOICEOVER:**
"Always segment! Targeted messages to specific customer groups perform 4 to 6 times better than mass blasts."

**3. Test and Learn**
**VOICEOVER:**
"Try different subject lines, send times, and offers. Track what works and do more of it."

**4. Create Urgency**
**VOICEOVER:**
"Time-limited offers convert better. 'Book by Friday' outperforms 'Book anytime.'"

**5. Make Response Easy**
**VOICEOVER:**
"Include multiple response options - reply YES, call this number, or click this link. Remove friction!"

**6. Track Everything**
**VOICEOVER:**
"Use the analytics. Learn from every campaign to improve the next one."

**[TEXT OVERLAY: "Pro Tips"]**

**[TEXT CALLOUTS:]**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm = best send times
- 15-25% discounts = sweet spot for offers
- SMS + Email together = maximum reach
- Preview before sending = avoid embarrassing mistakes!
