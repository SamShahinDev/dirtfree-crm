## General Questions

### What is Dirt Free CRM?

Dirt Free CRM is a comprehensive customer relationship management system designed specifically for carpet cleaning businesses. It handles customer management, job scheduling, technician dispatch, SMS communications, photo documentation, and customer surveys in one integrated platform.

### What browsers are supported?

- **Recommended**: Google Chrome (latest version)
- **Supported**: Firefox, Safari, Microsoft Edge (latest versions)
- **Mobile**: Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android

### Is the system available 24/7?

Yes, Dirt Free CRM is cloud-hosted and available 24/7. Planned maintenance windows are scheduled during off-peak hours and announced in advance.

### How do I get technical support?

1. **Check this FAQ** first for common solutions
2. **Contact your system administrator** for account issues
3. **Call dispatch** for operational support during business hours
4. **Email support** for non-urgent technical issues

## Account & Login

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

### Why can't I log in?

**Common causes:**
- Incorrect email or password
- Account has been deactivated
- Browser cache/cookies issues
- Caps Lock is on

**Solutions:**
1. Double-check your email and password
2. Try clearing browser cache and cookies
3. Use password reset if needed
4. Contact your administrator if account is locked

### My account seems locked. What should I do?

Accounts are temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or contact your administrator for immediate assistance.

### Can I change my email address?

Only system administrators can change user email addresses. Contact your admin with the request and they can update it for you.

## User Roles & Permissions

### What are the different user roles?

**Administrator:**
- Full system access
- User management
- System configuration
- Reports and audit logs

**Dispatcher:**
- Customer and job management
- Scheduling and assignments
- SMS communications
- Limited reporting

**Technician:**
- View assigned jobs only
- Update job status
- Upload photos
- Vehicle reporting

### Why can't I see certain features?

Your access is determined by your assigned role. If you need additional permissions, contact your system administrator to review your role assignment.

### Can I have multiple roles?

Users are assigned one primary role. If you need capabilities from multiple roles, your administrator can assign you the role with the highest level of access needed.

### How do I know what I can and cannot do?

Each role has specific permissions outlined in the training guides. Check your role-specific training documentation or ask your administrator.

## Customer Management

### How do I add a new customer?

1. Navigate to **Customers**
2. Click **"Add New Customer"**

5. Save the customer record

### I'm seeing duplicate customers. What should I do?

3. Use phone number search to find existing records

1. Search for and select the customer
2. Click **"Edit Customer"**

4. Save changes

### Can I delete a customer?

Customers with job history cannot be deleted, only deactivated. Contact your administrator for customer record management.

## Scheduling & Calendar

### How far in advance can I schedule jobs?

You can schedule jobs up to 6 months in advance. For recurring customers, longer-term scheduling may be available.

### Why does the system show scheduling conflicts?

The system detects conflicts like:
- Double-booked technicians
- Insufficient travel time between jobs
- Jobs outside service areas
- Technician skill/certification mismatches

### How do I resolve scheduling conflicts?

1. Review the conflict details
2. Adjust time, date, or technician assignment
3. Use suggested alternatives from the system
4. Confirm resolution addresses the conflict

### Can customers request specific technicians?

Yes, you can assign specific technicians to jobs. Note this in the customer preferences for future scheduling.

## SMS & Communication

### What are "quiet hours" and how do they work?

**Quiet Hours:** 9:00 PM - 8:00 AM Central Time

During quiet hours:
- Messages are automatically delayed until 8:00 AM CT
- Emergency messages can override with approval
- System ensures compliance with communication regulations

### How do I handle STOP requests?

When customers text STOP:
1. System automatically adds them to opt-out list
2. No further SMS messages will be sent
3. Use phone or email for future communications
4. Document the opt-out in customer notes

### Can customers opt back in after texting STOP?

Yes, customers can text START to re-enable SMS communications. The system will automatically remove them from the opt-out list.

### Why didn't my SMS send?

**Common reasons:**
- Phone number on opt-out list
- Invalid phone number format
- Quiet hours restriction
- Message content issues
- Account/service problems

### How do I send a manual SMS?

Use the reminders system or message templates. Never send SMS outside the system as it bypasses compliance controls.

## Surveys & Feedback

### How do customer surveys work?

1. Survey link sent automatically when job marked "Complete"
2. Customer receives SMS with unique survey link
3. Survey includes ratings and feedback questions
4. Results used for quality improvement

### What if a customer can't access their survey?

**Troubleshooting steps:**
1. Check if SMS was delivered
2. Verify survey link hasn't expired (30 days)
3. Confirm customer's phone number is correct
4. Dispatch can resend survey link

### How are survey responses handled?

- **4-5 stars:** Customer encouraged to leave public reviews
- **1-3 stars:** Management notified for follow-up
- All responses tracked for performance metrics

### Can surveys be customized?

Survey content and questions are configured by system administrators. Contact your admin for customization requests.

Dispatchers and administrators can adjust pricing when creating or editing jobs. Document reasons for changes.

### How do I handle customer payment questions?

1. Review job pricing in the system
2. Explain service breakdown to customer

4. Document any pricing discussions

### Are taxes included in pricing?

Tax calculation varies by location and is configured by administrators. Check with your admin for local tax settings.
