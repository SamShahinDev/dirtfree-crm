## AUTOMATED REVIEW REQUESTS

### Setup (One-Time)

1. Go to **Settings > Review Requests**
2. Toggle **Enable Automation** ON
3. Set timing: **24 hours** after job (recommended)
4. Choose delivery: **SMS + Email** (best response)
5. Select platforms: Google, Yelp, Facebook
6. Customize message template
7. Save settings

**Done!** System now auto-requests reviews after every completed job.

## RESPONDING TO POSITIVE REVIEWS (4-5 STARS)

### The Formula

1. **Thank them** by name
2. **Acknowledge** specific detail they mentioned
3. **Show personality** (be genuine, not robotic)
4. **Invite back** (soft CTA)

### Example Template
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! We're thrilled you loved
your [service type]. Our team takes pride in
delivering exceptional results. We appreciate your
business and look forward to serving you again soon!
```

### Key Points
✓ Use customer's name
✓ Reference specific service
✓ Show gratitude
✓ Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)
✓ Professional but friendly tone

---

## RESPONDING TO NEGATIVE REVIEWS (1-3 STARS)

### The Formula

1. **Thank** for feedback (shows maturity)
2. **Apologize** if appropriate (empathy)
3. **Address** concern briefly (don't over-explain)
4. **Take offline** (provide contact)
5. **Commit** to resolution

### Example Template
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We're sorry
your experience didn't meet expectations. We strive
for excellence and clearly fell short here. Please
contact our manager directly at [phone] so we can
make this right. We value your business and want
to ensure you're satisfied.
```

### GOLDEN RULES
✓ Stay calm and professional
✓ Don't argue or get defensive
✓ Apologize for experience (not admitting fault)
✓ Take conversation offline
✓ Offer to make it right
✓ Keep it brief
✓ Respond quickly

❌ Never argue publicly
❌ Don't blame customer
❌ Don't make excuses
❌ Don't ask to remove review
❌ Don't get emotional

## HANDLING DIFFICULT SITUATIONS

### Customer Threatens Negative Review

**DO:**
- Listen to their concerns
- Apologize sincerely
- Offer to resolve immediately
- Document everything
- Follow up after resolution

**DON'T:**
- Offer incentives to not leave review (illegal)
- Get defensive or argumentative
- Make promises you can't keep
- Ignore the threat

### Competitor Fake Review

1. Flag review through platform (spam/fake)
2. Respond professionally without accusations
3. Provide facts if appropriate
4. Request verification from platform
5. Focus on getting more real positive reviews

### Customer Wants Review Removed

**Response:**
"We appreciate your concern. While we can't remove
reviews (they're controlled by [Platform]), we'd love
the chance to make things right. Can we discuss how
to resolve this?"

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TIPS

### Google Reviews
- **Most important** for local SEO
- Respond to all reviews
- Use keywords naturally in responses
- Link to Google Business Profile in requests
- Monitor Google My Business regularly

### Yelp Reviews
- **Don't** ask customers to review on Yelp (against policy)
- **Do** respond to existing reviews
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Don't incentivize Yelp reviews
- Use Yelp for Business tools

### Facebook Reviews
- Enable reviews on business page
- Share positive reviews
- Respond publicly and professionally
- Use Facebook for customer service

## REVIEW GENERATION STRATEGIES

### After Every Job
- Technician mentions: "If you're happy, we'd love a review!"
- Leave card with QR code to review page
- Automated text 24 hours later
- Email follow-up if no response

### Review Campaigns
- Monthly focus on review generation
- Identify happy customers from past month
- Send personal review requests
- Offer loyalty points for review (100 points)
- Track campaign performance

### In-Person Requests
- "If everything looks great, would you mind leaving
  a quick review? I can text you the link right now."
- Show customer how easy it is
- Thank them regardless of answer
