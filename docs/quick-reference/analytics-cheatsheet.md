## PROMOTIONS EFFECTIVENESS

### Delivery Metrics

- **Sent**: Total messages queued
- **Delivered**: Successfully received (target: 95%+)
- **Failed**: Bounces/errors (keep <5%)

### Engagement Metrics

| Channel | Open Rate | Click/Response | Conversion |
|---------|-----------|---------------|------------|
| **Email** | 25-35% good, 35%+ excellent | 8-12% | 8-12% |
| **SMS** | 95%+ | 15-25% | 12-15% |
| **Both** | N/A | N/A | 12-18% |

### Campaign ROI

**Formula**: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100

**Benchmarks:**
- 300%+ ROI = Good
- 500%+ ROI = Excellent
- 1000%+ ROI = Outstanding

**Cost per Acquisition:**
- <$5 = Excellent
- $5-$10 = Good
- $10+ = Review and optimize

**Access**: Analytics > Promotions

## IMPORTANT RATIOS & FORMULAS

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
```
Total Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired
```
Target: <$50 per customer

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
```
Average Order Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan
```
Target: $1000-$2000+

### CLV:CAC Ratio
```
Customer Lifetime Value / Customer Acquisition Cost
```
Target: 3:1 or higher (3x return on acquisition spend)

### Retention Rate
```
(Customers at End - New Customers) / Customers at Start × 100
```
Target: 60-75%

### Churn Rate
```
Lost Customers / Total Customers at Start × 100
```
Target: <25% annually

### Revenue Per Customer
```
Total Revenue / Total Active Customers
```
Track month-over-month growth

## INTERPRETING TRENDS

### Revenue Trends

**Upward Trend** ✓
- Business growing
- Marketing working
- Continue current strategy

**Flat Trend** ⚠
- Stagnant growth
- Review pricing
- Increase marketing
- Improve retention

**Downward Trend** ❌
- Investigate immediately
- Check seasonality
- Review competition
- Examine customer feedback

### Customer Trends

**Retention Rate Dropping:**
- Improve service quality
- Increase follow-up
- Launch win-back campaigns
- Review pricing

**New Customers Declining:**
- Increase marketing spend
- Improve online presence
- Boost referral program
- Check review ratings
