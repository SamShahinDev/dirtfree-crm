## WHAT ARE PROMOTIONS?

Targeted marketing campaigns delivered via SMS and email to drive repeat business and stay top-of-mind.

**Key Benefits:**
- Re-engage inactive customers
- Drive repeat bookings
- Fill slow periods
- Reward loyal customers
- Track ROI precisely

---

## PROMOTION TYPES

| Type | Purpose | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Win-Back** | Re-engage inactive customers | Last service >6 months ago |
| **Birthday** | Celebrate customer birthdays | Birthday month special offers |
| **Seasonal** | Capitalize on seasons | Spring cleaning, holiday specials |
| **Anniversary** | Celebrate relationship | 1 year, 2 year milestones |
| **Referral** | Encourage referrals | Existing customer base |
| **Upsell** | Additional services | Recent customers |

## CREATING A PROMOTION (6 STEPS)

### STEP 1: Basic Details
- Click **Promotions** > **+ New Promotion**
- Enter promotion name (internal only)
- Select type from dropdown
- Write offer description

### STEP 2: Discount & Dates
- Set discount amount (% or $)
- Choose start date
- Choose end date

### STEP 3: Target Audience
- **All Customers** OR **Segment**
- Common segments:
  - Last service > 6 months (Win-back)
  - Birthday this month
  - High-value customers (>$1000 lifetime)
  - Specific zip codes
  - Service type history

### STEP 4: SMS Message
- Keep under 160 characters
- Include: Name, offer, deadline, CTA
- Example: "Hi [FirstName]! Spring special: 25% off carpet cleaning. Book by 4/30. Reply YES or call 555-1234."

### STEP 5: Email Message
- Compelling subject line (<60 chars)
- Rich content with images
- Clear call-to-action button
- Include terms/conditions

### STEP 6: Schedule & Send
- Send immediately OR schedule
- Choose delivery: SMS, Email, or Both
- Review estimated cost
- Click **Send** or **Schedule**

## OPTIMAL SEND TIMES

**Best Days**: Tuesday - Thursday
**Best Times**: 10am - 2pm local time
**Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends, holidays

---

## MESSAGE BEST PRACTICES

### SMS (160 characters max)

**DO:**
✓ Use customer's first name
✓ State offer clearly
✓ Include deadline for urgency
✓ Multiple response options (reply, call, click)
✓ Sign with business name

**DON'T:**
❌ Use all caps
❌ Multiple exclamation points!!!
❌ Unclear offers
❌ No call-to-action
❌ Too long (multiple messages)

### Email

**Subject Lines:**
✓ "[FirstName], we miss you! 25% off inside"
✓ "Happy Birthday [FirstName]! Special gift"
✓ "Last chance: Save 20% on carpet cleaning"

**Body:**
✓ Use responsive template
✓ Include compelling image
✓ Clear value proposition
✓ Prominent CTA button
✓ Terms and conditions
✓ Easy unsubscribe

## PERSONALIZATION TOKENS

Use these in messages to auto-populate customer data:

- `[FirstName]` - Customer's first name
- `[LastName]` - Customer's last name
- `[LastServiceDate]` - Last service date
- `[LifetimeValue]` - Total spent
- `[LoyaltyTier]` - Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum

**Example**:
"Hi [FirstName], as a valued [LoyaltyTier] member..."

## TRACKING PERFORMANCE

**Delivery Metrics:**
- Sent: Total messages
- Delivered: Successfully received
- Failed: Bounces/errors

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Open Rate: % who opened email
- Click Rate: % who clicked links
- Response Rate: % who replied (SMS)

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Bookings Generated
- Revenue Generated
- ROI: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
- Cost per Acquisition

**Access**: Promotions > Click campaign > View Details

## FREQUENCY GUIDELINES

**Maximum**: 1-2 promotions per month per customer
**Ideal Spacing**: 2+ weeks between campaigns
**Special Customers**: VIPs can receive more targeted offers

**Warning Signs of Over-Messaging:**
- Increasing opt-out rate
- Decreasing open rates
- Customer complaints
- Lower engagement over time

---

## SEGMENTATION IDEAS

**Win-Back Segments:**
- No service in 6-12 months
- No service in 12+ months (higher discount)

**VIP Campaigns:**
- Lifetime value >$2000
- Platinum tier members
- 5+ services in past year

**Geographic:**
- Specific neighborhoods
- Zip codes with availability
- New service areas

**Service-Based:**
- Carpet only (offer tile & grout)
- Tile only (offer carpet)
- One-time customers (encourage repeat)

## QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Promotion not sending | Check scheduled time, verify integrations active |
| Low open rates | Improve subject line, send at better time |
| High opt-out rate | Reduce frequency, improve targeting |
| Low conversion | Test different offers, improve targeting |
| Messages failing | Verify customer contact info, check integrations |

## A/B TESTING IDEAS

Test one variable at a time:

**Subject Lines:**
- Question vs. statement
- With emoji vs. without
- Urgency vs. benefit-focused
- Personalized vs. generic

**Offers:**
- 15% vs. 20% discount
- % off vs. $ off
- Free add-on vs. discount
- Limited time vs. ongoing

**Send Times:**
- Morning (10am) vs. afternoon (2pm)
- Tuesday vs. Thursday
- Immediate vs. 24-hour delay

**Channels:**
- SMS only vs. email only vs. both
