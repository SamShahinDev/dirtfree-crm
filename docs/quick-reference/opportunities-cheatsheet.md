## WHAT ARE OPPORTUNITIES?

Opportunities are potential sales that didn't convert immediately. Every "no" or "maybe" deserves follow-up!

**Common Types:**
- Service Declined - Customer chose not to proceed
- Price Objection - Customer mentioned cost concerns
- Postponed Booking - Customer wants to book later
- Partial Service - Only booked part of what was recommended

---

## CREATING AN OPPORTUNITY (5 STEPS)

### STEP 1: Navigate & Click
- Click **Opportunities** in sidebar
- Click **+ New Opportunity** button

### STEP 2: Fill Basic Info
- **Customer**: Search and select
- **Type**: Choose from dropdown
- **Estimated Value**: Potential revenue ($)
- **Reason**: Why didn't they book?

### STEP 3: Configure Auto-Offer (Optional)
- Toggle **Auto-send offer** ON
- Set discount % (15% works best!)
- Choose delivery: SMS, Email, or Both
- Set timing: 24 hours recommended

### STEP 4: Assign
- **Assigned to**: Team member
- **Follow-up date**: When to call if no response
- **Priority**: High, Medium, Low

### STEP 5: Save
- Click **Create Opportunity**
- Done! Auto-offer queues automatically

## PIPELINE STAGES

| Stage | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Pending** | New, awaiting action | Auto-offer preparing or needs manual contact |
| **Offer Sent** | Automated offer delivered | Wait for customer response, track engagement |
| **Follow-up Needed** | No response to offer | Call customer personally |
| **Negotiating** | Active discussions | Continue conversation, adjust offer if needed |
| **Converted** | WON! | Link to job, celebrate the win! |
| **Lost** | Didn't convert | Learn from it, move on |

**TIP**: Drag and drop cards between columns to update status

---

## CONVERTING AN OPPORTUNITY

When customer agrees to book:

1. Open opportunity card
2. Click **Convert to Booking**
3. Link to job (or create new job)
4. Enter actual value
5. Add conversion notes (what worked?)
6. Click **Confirm Conversion**

**Result**: Opportunity marked WON, revenue tracked, possible loyalty points awarded

---

## AUTO-OFFER BEST PRACTICES

| Element | Best Practice | Why |
|---------|--------------|-----|
| **Discount** | 15-20% | Sweet spot for conversion vs. profit |
| **Timing** | 24-48 hours | Lets them think, not forget |
| **Delivery** | SMS + Email | Maximum reach and engagement |
| **Message** | Personal, specific | Addresses their original concern |

## QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Auto-offer not sending | Check customer has valid SMS/email, verify integrations active |
| Can't convert opportunity | Verify opportunity status, ensure customer selected |
| Opportunity stuck in pipeline | Review and manually move or add follow-up note |
| Lost too many opportunities | Check lost reasons, adjust offers or process |

## KEY METRICS TO WATCH

- **Conversion Rate**: Target 25-35%
- **Average Deal Size**: Track trends
- **Time to Convert**: Faster is better
- **Offer Performance**: Which discounts work best?

**Access**: Analytics > Opportunities

## FOLLOW-UP SCRIPTS

**After Auto-Offer:**
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Dirt Free. I wanted to follow up on the special offer we sent yesterday. Do you have any questions I can answer?"

**Price Objection:**
"Hi [Name], I understand budget is a concern. Our current promotion brings the price to [new price], and we offer flexible payment options. Would you like to discuss?"

**Postponed Booking:**
"Hi [Name], you mentioned wanting to book later. We have availability [this week/next week]. Would any of these times work for you?"
