## HOW CUSTOMERS EARN POINTS

### Service Purchases (Primary)

**Formula**: Service Amount × Tier Multiplier = Points

**Examples:**
```
$300 service:
- Bronze: $300 × 1.0 = 300 points
- Silver: $300 × 1.25 = 375 points
- Gold: $300 × 1.5 = 450 points
- Platinum: $300 × 2.0 = 600 points
```

### Referrals
- **Customer refers friend**: 250 points when friend books
- **Friend benefits**: 10% off first service
- **Unlimited**: No cap on referrals

### Reviews
- **Google review**: 100 points
- **Yelp review**: 75 points
- **Facebook review**: 75 points
- **Limit**: One bonus per platform per year

### Service Milestones
- **1st anniversary**: 100 points
- **2nd anniversary**: 200 points
- **5th anniversary**: 500 points
- **10th anniversary**: 1000 points

### Special Promotions
- **Double points events**
- **Bonus point campaigns**
- **Holiday bonuses**
- **Manager discretion awards**

## MANUALLY AWARDING POINTS

### When to Use
- Service recovery/apology
- Special recognition
- Manager discretion
- Correct errors
- Promotional bonus

### How to Award

1. Navigate to **Loyalty > Members**
2. Search for customer
3. Click **Adjust Points**
4. Enter amount (+ or -)
5. Select reason from dropdown
6. Add detailed notes (required for audit)
7. Click **Award Points**

**Customer Notification**: Auto email + SMS with new balance

### Common Reasons
- **Service Recovery**: Issue resolution, apology
- **Special Promotion**: Campaign bonus
- **Manager Discretion**: Exceptional circumstances
- **Error Correction**: Fix system mistake
- **Anniversary Bonus**: Celebrate milestones

## REFERRAL PROGRAM

### How It Works

**Step 1: Customer Gets Code**
- Unique code in customer portal
- Example: JENNIFER-2024

**Step 2: Share with Friends**
- Copy link
- Email directly
- Text to contacts
- Post on social media

**Step 3: Friend Uses Code**
- Friend books service
- Enters code at booking
- Gets 10% off first service

**Step 4: Both Get Rewards**
- Referrer: 250 points
- Friend: 10% discount
- Win-win!

### Tracking Referrals

**Referral States:**
- **Pending**: Friend signed up, hasn't booked yet
- **Successful**: Friend completed first service
- **Points Awarded**: Referrer received 250 points

**Access**: Loyalty > Referrals

**Top Referrers**: Recognize and reward monthly

## TROUBLESHOOTING

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Points not awarded | Verify payment completed, check if job marked complete |
| Customer can't redeem | Check minimum balance (200 pts), verify reward available |
| Referral not tracking | Verify friend used code, check friend completed service |
| Wrong tier displayed | Recalculate points, verify tier thresholds correct |
| Customer wants refund | Deduct points earned from refunded transaction |

## PROGRAM POLICIES

### Points & Refunds

**If service refunded:**
- Points earned are deducted
- Points spent are refunded
- Automatic calculation

### Points Transfers

**Not Allowed:**
- Points cannot be transferred between customers
- Points cannot be sold or bartered
- Points are non-transferable

### Account Merging

**If duplicate accounts:**
- Contact support to merge
- Points combined
- Tier recalculated

### Fraud Prevention

**Warning Signs:**
- Multiple referrals from same IP
- Suspicious point redemptions
- Fake referral accounts
- Review gaming

**Action**: Flag for manager review, suspend if confirmed
